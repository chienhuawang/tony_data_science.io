LLM for Multi-Hop Q&A

Context: Existing LLMs struggle to accurately perform multi-hop question answering grounded in structured knowledge graphs, especially under Chinese-language contexts with limited fine-tuning support.

Method:

  • Designed and implemented a Graph-RAG architecture using Llamaindex's PropertyGraphIndex, integrated with OpenAl's GPT-3.5-turbo fine-tuning pipeline.
  • Programmatically generated high-quality JSONL training data combining contextual retrieval and manually curated golden responses.

Results: Successfully fine-tuned an LLM capable of precise knowledge graph reasoning and context-aware responses to multi-hop queries, supporting both Chinese and multilingual RAG systems and significantly improving answer accuracy and interpretability.
